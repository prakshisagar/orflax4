About Orflax

Our Story

ORFLAX INDIA PVT LTD is dedicated to excel in the research, development and production of wires and cables for electrical and other electronic applications. We are a North India based business group with a wide range of copper and aluminium wires & cables.

With over 15 years of experience, we have established ourselves as a leading manufacturer in the wire and cable industry, known for our commitment to quality and innovation.
